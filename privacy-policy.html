Privacy Policy for YouTube Paid Promotion Remover

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Data Collection

YouTube Paid Promotion Remover does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device and only modifies the visual appearance of YouTube pages to hide "Includes paid promotion" overlays.

2. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Used only to modify the appearance of YouTube pages by hiding promotional overlays.
  • youtube.com host permission: Required to detect and hide promotional overlays on YouTube pages.

3. Data Usage

This extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track user behavior
  • Does not communicate with external servers
  • Does not store any data locally

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services or analytics.
